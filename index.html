How to Use This Tracker

Getting Started

All district layers are off by default — use the toggle buttons in the top-right corner to turn them on.

Toggling District Layers

Click any of the three buttons to show or hide that layer on the map:

  • Congressional — U.S. House districts
  • Senate — NY State Senate districts
  • Assembly — NY State Assembly districts

You can have multiple layers on at once. Click Reset to turn all layers off and clear your selection.

Exploring a District

Once a layer is on, click any district on the map to select it. The side panel on the left will show:

  • The district number and current representative (if any)
  • A list of candidates running in that race
  • Each candidate's name, party, and any endorsements they've received

Browsing Endorsers

Click on an endorser's name in the candidate list to switch into Endorser Mode. The panel will show every race that endorser has made an endorsement in, and the map will highlight any districts with at least one endorsed candidate.

Click the endorser's name again (or hit Reset) to exit Endorser Mode.

Using Search

You can search for:

  • A district by number (e.g. "Assembly District 47" or "Senate District 12")
  • A candidate's name
  • An endorsing organization or individual

Clicking a search result jumps straight to that district or pulls up that endorser's full endorsement profile.
